Installation Process

4 steps. 2–3 days.
Job done.

Most installs are completed within 2–3 days, even on large projects. No specialist trades needed — just one crew who knows the system.

01

Fix plates & end studs

Secure the bottom and top plates, plus one stud each end of the wall run.

02

Install panels — one side

Lock panels in place on the first face. Screw top and bottom only. No complicated fastening.

03

Repeat & finish

Install the opposite face, then fit skirting and scotia. Clean, fast, zero mess.

04

Ready for handover

Walls are finished, painted, and ready. No waiting on trades. Your project moves forward immediately.
